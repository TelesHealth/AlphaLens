Extend fetchFedCutProbability() in
artifacts/api-server/src/services/kalshi-markets.ts
to calculate cumulative cut probability across all
KXFED events through July 2026, not just the
nearest meeting.

The FED-CUT asset is titled "Fed Rate Cut by July
2026" — so the correct market price is the
probability of at least one cut occurring at ANY
FOMC meeting between now and July 2026.

Probability math:
  P(at least one cut by July) =
  1 - P(no cut at meeting 1) × P(no cut at meeting 2) × ...

Where P(no cut at meeting N) = the YES price of
the contract at the current rate threshold for
that meeting's KXFED event.

Implementation:

1. Fetch all open KXFED events (not just nearest).
   Group markets by event_ticker.
   Filter events where close_time is before
   August 1, 2026 (covers all meetings through July).

2. For each qualifying event, find the contract
   at the current rate threshold (targetUpper - 0.25)
   using the same logic as the existing single-event
   calculation.
   
   Extract holdProbability = YES price / 100
   (probability Fed holds at that meeting)

3. Calculate cumulative cut probability:
   cumulativeHoldProb = holdProbabilities.reduce(
     (acc, p) => acc * p, 1
   )
   cutProbability = Math.round(
     (1 - cumulativeHoldProb) * 100
   )

4. Update the log line:
   "Kalshi KXFED: cumulative cut probability by
   July 2026 = {X}% across {N} FOMC events
   (hold probs: {p1}%, {p2}%, ...)"

5. Fall back to single-event calculation if fewer
   than 2 events are found before August 2026.

6. Fall back to null if calculation fails entirely.
